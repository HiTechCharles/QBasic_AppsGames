keyboard.  You can do all commands with one hand.  Here's the layout

#1:  Press '1' when someone gets a bingo.  You will be asked if the called
bingo is good.  If there is a bingo, answer yes.  If there is no legal
bingo, press N.  You will still see the current number on-screen.

#2:  Hit '2' to show different bingo patterns.  These patterns show you
different ways you can get a bingo.  While viewing the patterns, press the UP
or DOWN arrows to see the entire document.  Press ESCape to return to the
game with the current number on the screen.

#3:  This command simply quits the program.  
ESCape can also be pressed to quit.

#0:  You've got this command to work.  During the game, press 0 to show this
document again.  Press UP or DOWN to scroll through the document; ESCape
will close the document and return you to the game in progress with the 
current number showing.

. (period):  Press the period key to hear the current number out loud again.
  (This will only work with the "sound" version of this program.

ENTER:  Press ENTER to call the next number.  The next number will 
appear as it is spoken.  (Spoken allowed in "sound" version only.)


THE SCREEN

The screen is divided into two major areas during normal play.  The area on 
the left, in the middle of the screen, shows you the current number called.  
The area on the right is the "calling card."  This panel shows you all of 
the numbers that have been called for each letter.  Just like the 
professionals!  A line at the bottom left of your screen shows the total 
number of called numbers.
